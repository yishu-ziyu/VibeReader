# VibeReader Demo Note

VibeReader is a local-first reading workspace for papers, notes, and web articles.

## Core Loop

- Open a document from disk.
- Read on the left side.
- Ask the AI assistant on the right side.
- Turn useful passages into summaries, flashcards, mind maps, or annotations.

## Demo Passage

The important design decision is that the reader and assistant are visible at the same time. This keeps the user's attention anchored in the source document while allowing fast questions, synthesis, and review.

```text
Read, select, ask, save.
```
